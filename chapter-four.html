The two boys walk down a well worn path that weaves between trees that almost seem autonomous in their pairing. Down the corridor of trees they go, walking faster between slower moving festival goers. Piotrs goose is getting distressed. He's been holding it this whole time, he may have clutched it to hard during Romas story and it got a little quiet, but it's awake now. The goose is certainly awake, and honking like crazy now.
